UCS Venator

The Venator-class Star Destroyer, also known as a Republic attack cruiser or Jedi Cruiser, was a dagger-shaped capital ship used by the Galactic Republic Navy for ship-to-ship combat. It was the predecessor of the Imperial-class Star Destroyer.

Features

1,768 Pieces

With 1,768 pieces, this LEGO Star Wars starship construction toy makes a great birthday gift, holiday present or special surprise for kids aged 9 and up and any Star Wars fan. It’s easy to build and will provide hours of fun.

Display Stand

The Venator-class Star Destroyer measures 8.5” (22cm) high, 22” (57cm) long and 18.5” (48cm) wide. It makes a striking display piece in any child’s bedroom between playtime adventures.

Mini Figures

There are lots of LEGO Star Wars sets to delight fans of all ages, whether they want to recreate famous scenes, role-play their own stories or simply build and display the authentic replica construction models.
